About

My Story

I am a UX Designer living in Boston, MA. My educational background spans psychology, anthropology, and public health, allowing me to delve into the intricate connection between our upbringing and the decisions we make. My academic journey involved analyzing how our upbringing is influenced by these factors, while also exploring the profound impact of culture and environment on our overall health. What fascinates me is the significant overlap between comprehending decision-making in health and design. My passion lies in UX research, where the primary objective is to gain insight into the thought processes of our users and develop structures that are not only intuitive but also align with how they think.
